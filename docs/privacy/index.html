Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Overview

TennisCue ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password when you create an account
  • Practice data: Your skill level, cue selections, session ratings, and practice notes

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the TennisCue experience:

  • Personalizing your practice cues based on skill level
  • Syncing your progress across devices
  • Tracking your practice history and session ratings

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which provides encrypted data storage and authentication. Data is stored on servers located in the United States.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and cloud data storage

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any other third parties.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at info@laceup.club. We will process your request within 30 days.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@laceup.club.
